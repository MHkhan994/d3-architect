Our Team

Our team is composed of highly skilled and experienced architects, designers, and engineers who are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of traditional architecture and creating unique and functional buildings.

BIM/CAD Specialist

BIM/CAD Specialists are responsible for creating and maintaining digital building models using Building Information Modeling (BIM) software. They ensure that the models are accurate, up-to-date, and comply with industry standards.

John Smith

Project Manager

Project Managers oversee the entire project, ensuring it stays on schedule, within budget and meets the client's requirements. They coordinate the work of the design team and contractors to ensure a successful delivery.

Jessica Green

Interior Designer

Interior designers are responsible for creating functional and visually appealing interior spaces. They work with architects, builders and clients to determine the design style of a space, as well as materials and furniture to create a cohesive and visually pleasing design.

Christopher Davis

Job Captain

A Job Captain leads the production of construction documents, coordinates with the design team and ensures the project stays on schedule and within budget. They also review and approve drawings and ensure compliance with building codes and regulations.

David Kim

Principal Architect

The Principal Architect leads the design team, oversees project development, and represents the firm to clients. They make important decisions for the firm and provide guidance to junior staff members.
